Who We Are

Built on Integrity, Powered by Data

Apext was founded in Carlisle with a singular mission: to give institutional decision-makers information superiority without conflicts of interest. Today we support mandates across EMEA, the Americas, and Asia-Pacific from our UK headquarters.

Our heritage. Beginning as a boutique research desk serving North West England fund managers, Apext evolved into a full-spectrum financial information consultancy. We remain privately held and client-funded — never beholden to sell-side distribution.

Our philosophy. Markets reward clarity. Every Apext engagement is structured around your decision calendar: what you need to know, when you need to know it, and how it changes your risk posture.

Our commitment. We adhere to FCA-aligned advisory frameworks, maintain strict information barriers, and publish methodology notes alongside every major deliverable.

Expand each pillar to learn more

Our analysts carry no product distribution responsibilities. Research outputs are peer-reviewed internally and version-controlled for audit trails. Clients receive source attribution, confidence scoring, and explicit limitation statements on every report.
From MiFID II transaction reporting to UK EMIR equivalence updates, Apext translates regulation into operational checklists. We maintain living policy trackers and alert clients within hours of material supervisory publications.
Secure client portals, API feeds, and scheduled PDF briefings ensure intelligence reaches the right stakeholders. Custom dashboards integrate with your existing BI stack — Tableau, Power BI, or internal warehouses.
ESG is embedded across our research stack, not siloed. SFDR classification support, transition risk modelling, and greenwashing detection frameworks are available as modular add-ons to any core mandate.

Regulatory & Compliance Advisory

Navigate UK and EU regulatory change with confidence. Apext interprets supervisory guidance into actionable policies, control matrices, and board papers — reducing reliance on generic law-firm summaries.

  • MiFID II / UK onshoring gap assessments
  • EMIR clearing obligation monitoring
  • Consumer Duty impact mapping for wealth managers
  • Regulatory horizon scanning quarterly

Risk Analytics & Stress Testing

Institutional-grade risk frameworks without enterprise software lock-in. We build reproducible models in your environment, document assumptions transparently, and facilitate ICAAP/ILAAP dialogue preparation.

  • VaR / ES backtesting & model validation support
  • Liquidity coverage scenario libraries
  • Climate stress overlays (NGFS pathways)
  • Counterparty concentration dashboards

Corporate Finance Intelligence

Support CFOs and strategy teams with timing intelligence for capital markets activity. Peer benchmarking, multiples analysis, and debt capacity modelling inform your next issuance or M&A move.

  • Synergy validation for acquisition targets

ESG & Sustainable Finance

Meet investor and regulator expectations with defensible ESG integration. Our methodologies align with TCFD, ISSB, and SFDR while flagging data quality issues that undermine portfolio claims.

  • Portfolio carbon footprint & trajectory analysis

  • Green bond framework second opinions
  • Biodiversity risk screening (TNFD-aligned)
